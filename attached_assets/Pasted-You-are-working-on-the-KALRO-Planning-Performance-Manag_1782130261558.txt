You are working on the KALRO Planning, Performance Management (PPM) System. Focus exclusively on the **Projects module** and modify the Strategy Mapping workflow.

Implement the following changes:

### 1. Strategy Mapping Hierarchy Display

When a user selects a **Key Result Area (KRA)**, automatically display all related Strategic Objectives and their associated Strategies grouped under that same KRA.

Example:

```text
☑ KRA 1

Strategic Objectives

• Strategic Objective A
    ○ Strategy 1
    ○ Strategy 2

• Strategic Objective B
    ○ Strategy 3
    ○ Strategy 4
```

Requirements:

* Strategic Objectives must appear under the selected KRA.
* Strategies must appear under their respective Strategic Objective.
* The entire hierarchy must remain grouped under one KRA container/card/section.
* Do not separate Strategic Objectives and Strategies into different unrelated sections.

---

### 2. Progressive Strategy Selection Workflow

When a user clicks or selects a Strategy, dynamically display the related Key Activities as checkboxes.

Example:

```text
Strategy 1

☐ Key Activity 2
☐ Key Activity 3
```

```

```

### 5. Single Hierarchical View

All information must remain visible under the same selected Key Result Area.

Hierarchy:

```text
Key Result Area
    ↓
Strategic Objective
    ↓
Strategy
    ↓
Key Activity
    ↓
Expected Output
    ↓
Output Indicator
```

Requirements:

* Do not open new pages.
* Do not open modals.
* Do not create separate panels for each level.
* Dynamically expand each level beneath its parent selection.
* Maintain a clear tree/hierarchy structure.
* Allow multiple selections where applicable.
* Preserve all existing relationships and data integrity.

### Final Expected Result

When a KRA is selected, users can progressively drill down through:

* Strategic Objectives
* Strategies
* Key Activities
* Expected Outputs
* Output Indicators

all within a single hierarchical interface under the selected Key Result Area, making it easy to visualize and manage the complete planning structure.
